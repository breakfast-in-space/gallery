Breakfast in Space is a multidisciplinary creative practice working across photography, music production, and visual art. This gallery collects work made in the margins — images, sounds, and objects that exist somewhere between intention and chance, between the studio and the world outside it.

The work gathered here resists easy categorization. Photography bleeds into visual art; studio process becomes its own kind of document. What connects it is an attention to the texture of experience — the way light behaves in a room, the way silence carries weight, the way material pushes back.
